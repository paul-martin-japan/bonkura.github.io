"diffugere"  flee in different directions, disperse, scatter, disappear This is a clear example of the Latin present perfect: "the snows have disappeared;  the grasses are returning."
"vices"  (1) change, interchange, alternation, vicissitude (2) fate, lot, condition, fortune

IACKSON HARVM LITERARVM CONTEMPTORI)..
